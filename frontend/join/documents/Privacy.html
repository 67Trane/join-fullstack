COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA WHEN VISITING OUR WEBSITE

(1) If you use the website for informational purposes only, i.e., if you do not send us any other information, we only collect the personal data your browser transmits to our server. If you want to view our website, we collect the following data, which is technically necessary for us to display our website to you and to ensure stability and security (the legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR):

  • IP address,
  • Date and time of the request,
  • Time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT),
  • Content of the request (specific page),
  • Access status/HTTP status code,
  • Amount of data transferred,
  • Website from which the request originates,
  • Browser,
  • Operating system and its interface,
  • Language and version of the browser software.

(2) In addition to the aforementioned data, cookies may be stored on your computer when you use our website—either now or in the future. Cookies are small text files stored on your hard drive and assigned to the browser you use, through which the entity that sets the cookie (in this case, us) receives certain information. Cookies cannot run programs or transmit viruses to your computer. They serve to make the internet offer more user-friendly and effective overall (the legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. a GDPR). You can generally delete or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. We may also use technologies known as Local Storage or Session Storage, which allow data to be stored locally in your browser’s cache. This data remains even after closing the browser window or shutting down the program unless you manually clear the cache. Local storage helps save your preferences while using our website. Session Storage is similar but only retains data for the browser session. No third parties can access the data stored in Local or Session Storage. This data will not be shared with third parties or used for advertising purposes. These technologies are used, for instance, to present our content in an appealing graphic format (e.g., pop-up windows) and personalize our offering for easier navigation.

(3) We primarily use these techniques to improve the user experience and functionality of our websites. We do so based on our legitimate interest in providing a fully functional, attractive offering, per Article 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR. Where consent is required for the use of cookies, we will ask for your consent at the appropriate place. If you do not wish these techniques to be used, you can disable them in your browser settings.
